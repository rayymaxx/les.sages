For licensed practitioners

Your caseload, without the paperwork holding it back.

Therape brings clients to you from districts you couldn't otherwise reach, and clears the scheduling and billing work off your desk so more of your time goes to the room, not the receipt book.

Reach peri-urban districts

Our USSD sector-matching sends you clients from areas with no walk-in access to a therapist today, without you needing a second office.

Automated scheduling

Clients book directly into your open slots, whether they arrive from the Web App or an in-person booking code. No back-and-forth calls.

Built-in billing

Session fees are collected and settled automatically on a flat model — no chasing invoices, no manual reconciliation.

Financial transparency

One flat fee. No surprises, for you or your client.

Every session on Therape is priced the same way, regardless of channel: a flat RWF 8,000. It's deliberately simple — clients know what to expect before they book, and you know what to expect before you accept.

Sessions booked by low-income clients through the offline stream can qualify for a subsidized rate; the difference is covered by Therape's partner fund, not deducted from your payout.
