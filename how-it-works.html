Online stream

AI Companion Privacy & Anonymity Guardrails

The AI companion is a support tool between sessions, not a replacement for your therapist, and it's built with limits designed to protect you:

  • •Conversations with the companion are never linked to your real name — your profile uses an anonymous session ID only.
  • •Nothing you tell the companion is shown to your human therapist unless you explicitly choose to share it.
  • •Chats are never used to train external models and are not sold or shared with advertisers.
  • •If the companion detects language suggesting immediate danger, it will surface local crisis resources and offer to connect you to a human counselor — it will not silently log or escalate without telling you.
  • •You can delete your companion history at any time from account settings, permanently.

Offline stream

USSD Grassroots Data Security & Gossip Protection

USSD runs over a shared phone, a shared line, and sometimes a shared household. We designed around that reality directly:

  • •Nothing about a session — not that it happened, not its content — is written to the phone's call log or message history.
  • •Health post booking codes carry a reference number only. Front-desk staff can confirm a valid booking without ever seeing your name or your reason for the visit.
  • •Sector-level counselor matching uses your location tier only, never a precise address, and it's discarded after the session routes.
  • •Counselors on the toll-free line are trained specifically on confidentiality in small communities — including how to keep a session unremarkable to anyone nearby.
  • •No USSD session data is ever shared with telecom partners beyond what's required to route the free call.
